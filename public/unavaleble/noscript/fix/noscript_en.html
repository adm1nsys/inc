Other Problems For Developers

Safari (Mac)

  1. On the web browser menu click on the "Edit" or "Safari" and select "Preferences" or "Settings". You can also use the key combination "CMD" + ",".
  2. In the "Preferences" window select the "Security" tab.
  3. In the "Security" tab section "Web content" mark the "Enable JavaScript" checkbox.
  4. Click on the "Reload the current page" button of the web browser to refresh the page.

Safari (iOS/iPad OS)

  1. Go in the Settings app.
  2. Click on Safari.
  3. Click on Advanced.
  4. Torn on JavaScript.
  5. Reload page in browser.
